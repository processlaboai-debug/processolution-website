How It Works

Three steps. Five seconds.

No forms, no typing, no friction. Just speak — Task Coordinator handles everything else.

🎙️
1

Speak naturally

Tap the microphone and say the task the way you'd tell a colleague: "Remind Sara to send the contract to ABC Corp by Friday, high priority."

🤖
2

AI structures it

Gemini AI instantly extracts title, assignee, deadline, priority, and description into structured fields. Review and confirm in one tap.

📧
3

Team gets notified

Your assignee receives an email with action buttons — Accept, Start, Complete — plus a calendar invite. No login required on their end.

Features

Built for the field,
not the office.

Every feature exists to save you time — whether you're on a construction site, between meetings, or managing a multilingual team.

🎙️

Voice Input & AI Parsing

Speak in any of 25 languages. Gemini 2.5 extracts every field automatically and learns your vocabulary over time.

  • One-tap recording
  • Natural language deadlines ("next Monday", "end of week")
  • Per-user voice learning & custom dictionary
  • Manual edit fallback always available
🌍

25 Languages

All 24 official EU languages plus Norwegian. Voice, UI, emails, and task responses — all fully localized.

  • Natural localization, not literal translation
  • Switch language anytime from the header
  • Built-in translation feedback tool
📧

Email Notifications & Actions

Assignees get an email the moment a task is created — with action buttons they can click without logging in.

  • Accept · Start · Complete · Decline — all from email
  • Calendar invite (.ics) attached automatically
  • Scheduled reminders before deadlines
  • Works with Gmail, Outlook, or any SMTP
📋

Full Task Management

A complete workflow from creation to archive — with filters, search, and a live dashboard.

  • Status: Pending → In Progress → Completed
  • Priority color-coding: High / Medium / Low
  • Quick filters, full-text search, date ranges
  • Notes & full activity log per task
🔄

Two-Level Delegation

Forward a task to a colleague while keeping a link to the original. Stay informed without micromanaging.

  • Delegated tasks visible with a clear badge
  • Recipients can act even without an account
  • Original sender stays updated throughout
📱

Mobile-First & PWA

Designed for phones first — installable as an app on iOS, Android, and desktop with offline support.

  • Progressive Web App — no app store required
  • Native iOS, Android, Mac builds via Capacitor
  • Works offline for previously loaded tasks
  • Dark theme for low-light use
📊

Dashboard & Export

Live stat cards for your current workload. Export any filtered view to Excel or CSV in one click.

  • Pending / In Progress / Completed / Archived
  • Click any card to filter the task list instantly
  • Export to .xlsx or .csv — ready for reporting
🔒

Security & Privacy

GDPR-aligned from the ground up. Your data is yours — and only yours.

  • EU-hosted backend
  • Row-level security — no user sees another's data
  • Full data export & one-click account deletion
  • Secure tokenized public links with expiry
👥

Teams & Multi-Tenancy

Built for individuals and teams alike. Invite members, manage roles, and share task pools.

  • Google Sign-In or email + password
  • Invite team members by email
  • Smart contact auto-save from voice input
  • Multiple email addresses per contact
